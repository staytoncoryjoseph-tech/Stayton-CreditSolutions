Frequently Asked Questions

How long does credit repair take?

Most clients see changes within 30–90 days, but timing depends on bureaus, furnishers, and your file.

Is credit repair legal?

Yes. Federal and Texas laws allow consumers to dispute inaccurate, unverifiable, or outdated information.

Do you guarantee score increases?

No legitimate firm can guarantee a specific score outcome. We guarantee compliant work and transparent service.

What if I have identity theft?

We help you assemble an identity theft packet and escalate with bureaus, furnishers, and law enforcement when needed.
